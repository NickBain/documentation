## Extracted Data Fields

VATextract extracts comprehensive invoice data including:

- **Header fields**: Invoice number, dates (invoice, due, delivery), purchase order
- **Financial data**: Net amount, VAT amount, VAT rate, total, currency
- **Supplier details**: Name, address, VAT/Tax ID, contact info
- **Receiver details**: Name, address, VAT/Tax ID
- **Line items**: Description, quantity, unit price, amount, product codes
